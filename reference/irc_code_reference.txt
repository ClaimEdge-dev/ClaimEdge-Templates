IRC R905.2.1
Requirement: Asphalt shingles must be fastened to solidly sheathed decks
When It Applies: Every shingle roof replacement
Adjuster Rebuttal: "The deck is compromised — I can't fasten to it per R905.2.1"

IRC R905.2.2
Requirement: Underlayment required beneath shingles (single or double per slope)
When It Applies: All roof replacements
Adjuster Rebuttal: "Underlayment is code-required, not optional"

IRC R905.1.1
Requirement: Ice barrier extending 24" inside exterior wall line at eaves
When It Applies: Heated structures in cold climate zones
Adjuster Rebuttal: "[CITY] requires ice barrier per adopted IRC"

IRC R905.2.8.3
Requirement: Drip edge required at eaves and rakes (1/4" projection, 2" up-roof)
When It Applies: All roof replacements
Adjuster Rebuttal: "Drip edge is code, not an upgrade"

IRC R905.2.8
Requirement: Flashing required at valleys, walls, chimneys, penetrations
When It Applies: All roof replacements
Adjuster Rebuttal: "Flashing is code-required weather protection"

IRC R908.3
Requirement: Full slope replacement when damage exceeds 25% of area
When It Applies: Wind/hail damage assessments
Adjuster Rebuttal: "We're over 25% — patching violates R908.3"

IRC R806.2
Requirement: Attic ventilation: 1:150 ratio (or 1:300 balanced)
When It Applies: Enclosed attic spaces
Adjuster Rebuttal: "Ventilation is code, not an upgrade"

IRC R806.3
Requirement: Ventilation openings protected against rain/snow
When It Applies: All vent installations

Matching Doctrine — Line v. State Farm
Principle: When damaged materials can't be matched, carrier must replace connected sections for uniform appearance

Matching Doctrine — Adams v. Cameron Mutual
Principle: "Like kind and quality" requires visual uniformity, not just functional equivalence

Reasonable Expectations Doctrine
Principle: Policyholder is entitled to expect replacement restores pre-loss appearance
